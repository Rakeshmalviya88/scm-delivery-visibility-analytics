# 4. Dashboard KPIs and Insights

## KPI Cards
- On-Time Delivery %
- Average Delay (minutes)
- Average Cost Overrun (actual - planned)
- Predicted High-Risk Share

## Visualizations
- Weekly average delay trend.
- Carrier performance comparison (on-time vs average cost).
- Route risk scatter using predicted delay risk.
- Monthly shipment trend with one-step forecast.

## Interactive Controls
- Month filter.
- Carrier filter.

## Example Executive Insights
- Identify top risk routes requiring dispatch buffer adjustments.
- Identify carriers with high cost but low on-time performance.
- Compare seasonal delay shifts to tune capacity and planning.
- Quantify impact of operational factors (weather and traffic).
